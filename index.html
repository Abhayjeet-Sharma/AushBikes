About Us

Built on the floor,
not just on paper.

A short overview of who you are, what you make, and why workshops and riders choose you.

AUSH Bikes has been manufacturing bicycle components for over 4 years, supplying workshops, distributors and OEM partners with parts built to exact tolerance. Every component that leaves our facility is machined, tested and inspected in-house before it ships.

We work across the full range of drivetrain and running-gear parts — chains, hubs, cranksets, brake systems and more — using material sourced and tested to consistent, documented standards. It's the kind of consistency that shops can build a reputation on.
